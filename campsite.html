Camping at the heart of the National Park

Great Make-Believe Campsite is the nature loversâ€™ campsite in the very heart of the National Park. This is an ideal mountain base camp in the most beautiful valley in Wales. You can walk up the mountain directly from the site or simply enjoy natural beauty by the lake and river, relaxing by your own camp fire. The site can be busy in major holidays, but no sound systems are allowed and quiet is required after 11 pm. Bookings are now required for all campers, including tent pitches without electric, see our Booking page.
